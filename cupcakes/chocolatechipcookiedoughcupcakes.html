chocolate chip cookie dough cupcakes

vanilla cupcake stuffed with chocolate chip cookie dough and topped with vanilla buttercream frosting

recipe from Life in the Lofthouse


baked in May 2015

yield: 24 cupcakes
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 16 minutes
difficulty level: medium

Ingredients

Cookie Dough

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 4 Tablespoons milk
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips

Cupcakes

  • 3 sticks unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 and 1/2 cups brown sugar, packed
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 and 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

Frosting

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • 3-4 Tablespoons half & half (or milk)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

For the cookie dough:

  1. Combine butter and both sugars in a mixing bowl and cream on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
  2. Beat in milk and vanilla until incorporated and smooth.
  3. Beat in the flour and salt until just combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop about 1 and 1/2 Tablespoons of dough at a time, and shape into balls. Place cookie dough balls on a parchment or foil lined baking sheet.
  6. Cover with plastic wrap and freeze overnight.

For the cupcakes:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two cupcake pans with paper liners (24 total).
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, combine the butter and brown sugar. Beat together on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
  3. Mix in the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition, and scraping down the sides of bowl as needed.
  4. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a medium bowl. Stir together to blend.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the mixer bowl on low speed, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix each addition just until incorporated.
  6. Blend in the vanilla.
  7. Fill the cupcake liners about 2/3 full with the cupcake batter. Place frozen cookie dough ball on the top center of each cupcake.
  8. Bake for 16-18 minutes.

For the frosting:

  1. Place butter in a large mixing bowl. Beat on medium speed until creamy.
  2. Slowly add in a powdered sugar one cup at a time, beating on medium speed until smooth.
  3. Pour in half & half, salt and vanilla, and continue to beat until smooth and combined.
  4. Frost cooled cupcakes and sprinkle with mini chocolate chips.
