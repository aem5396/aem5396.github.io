apple cinnamon oatmeal cookies

cinnamon oatmeal cookies with shreadded apples and chopped walnuts

recipe from Sally's Cookie Addiction cookbook


baked in July 2022

yield: 30 cookies
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 12 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients
  • 1 cup shredded peeled apple (about 2 apples)
  • 1 and 1/2 cups old-fashioned whole rolled oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions
  1. Gently blot the shredded apple with a paper towel to remove some of the moisture. Set aside.
  2. Whisk the oats, flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, using a handheld mixer or a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat the butter on medium-high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the brown sugar and granulated sugar, and beat on medium-high speed until creamed, about 2 minutes.
  5. Add the egg, vanilla extract, and milk, and beat on high speed until combined, about 1 minute.Scrape down the sides and up the bottom of the bowl and beat again as needed to combine.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix on low speeduntil combined.
  7. With the mixer running on low speed, beat in the apple and walnuts. The dough will be thick and sticky.
  8. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.


  9. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.
  10. Scoop balls of dough, about 1 and 1/2 Tablespoons of dough per cookie, and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheets.
  11. Bake for 12 to 13 minutes, or until lightly browned on the sides. The centers will look very soft.
  12. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
