cream wafers

buttery and flaky cookie sandwiches with buttercream filling

recipe from Betty Crocker


baked in January 2025

yield: 30 cookies
prep time: 45 minutes
cook time: 9 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • granulated sugar, for coating

Filling

  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Food color, if desired

Directions

For the cookies:

  1. In medium bowl, mix flour, 1 cup butter and the whipping cream with spoon. Cover and refrigerate about 1 hour or until firm.

  2. Heat oven to 375°F.
  3. Roll one-third of dough at a time 1/8 inch thick on lightly floured surface. (Keep remaining dough refrigerated until ready to roll.)
  4. Cut into desired shapes with 1 and 1/2-inch cookie cutters.
  5. Generously cover large piece of waxed paper with granulated sugar.
  6. Transfer cutouts to waxed paper, using pancake turner. Turn each cutout to coat both sides.
  7. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Prick each cutout with fork about 4 times.
  8. Bake 7 to 9 minutes or just until set but not brown.
  9. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely, about 1 hour.

For the filling:

  1. Mix all creamy filling ingredients until smooth. Add a few drops water if necessary.
  2. Spread about 1/2 teaspoon filling between bottoms of pairs of cookies.
