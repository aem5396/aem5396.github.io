holly clusters

classic cornflake marshmallow holly cluster christmas cookies

recipe from Recipe Girl


baked in December 2020

yield: 24 cookies
prep time: 25 minutes
cook time: 10 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
  • 30 large (regular) marshmallows
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons green food coloring
  • 3 and 1/2 cups corn flakes
  • red cinnamon candies

Directions
  1. In a large pot, melt the butter and marshmallows over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  2. Add the vanilla and food coloring.
  3. Stir in the corn flakes.
  4. Grease a spoon and drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto a greased cookie sheet, or vegetable oil sprayed wax paper.
  5. Place 3 red cinnamon candies on each bunch of "holly," pressing slightly so they stick.
  6. Let sit until they have cooled and set.
