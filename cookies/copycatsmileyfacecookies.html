copycat smiley face cookies

soft sugar cookies with sweet vanilla icing and smiley face decoration

recipe from Chef Success


baked in July 2023

yield: 30 cookies
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 8 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter-flavored shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 cups flour, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Icing

  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons milk
  • 3 Tablespoons light corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • gel food coloring

Directions

For the cookies:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream sugar and shortening together.
  2. Add eggs and mix until conbined.
  3. Add 1 cup flour, soda, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Combine milk and vanilla.
  5. Add milk/vanilla to creamed mixture alternating with remaining 4 cups flour.
  6. Cover and chill at least 1 hour.

  7. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  8. Roll dough onto lightly floured surface 1/4 inch thick.
  9. Use cookie-cutters to cut dough into large circles.
  10. Bake on an ungreased cookie sheetsfor 8 to 10 minutes, or until edges just begin to turn light golden brown.

For the icing:

  1. Add all of the incredients into a large bowl and mix until icing is smooth and glossy. If icing is too thick, add more corn syrup.
  2. Divide the icing into separate bowls and stir in the food coloring as desired.
  3. Spread the base color icing in a circle over the cookies. Let dry at least 30 minutes.
  4. Use other color icing to pipe smiley faces on the cookies.
  5. Icing could take one full day to fully set.
