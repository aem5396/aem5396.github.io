hermits

spiced oatmeal cookies with craisins and chopped walnuts

family recipe


baked in April 2024

yield: 32 cookies
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 15 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients
  • 1 cup butter, at room temperature
  • 2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup cold coffee
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoon cloves
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 and 1/2 cups raisins and/or craisins
  • 1 and 1/4 cups broken walnuts

Directions
  1. Using the flat attachment, mix together the butter, brown sugar, and eggs.
  2. Mix in the coffee and vanilla.
  3. Combine flour oatmeal, baking soda, salt, and spices together.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mix.
  5. Mix in craisins and nuts.
  6. Chill dough in the refrigerator at least one hour, preferably overnight.

  7. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  8. Drop walnut size spoons of dough on cookie sheet.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
