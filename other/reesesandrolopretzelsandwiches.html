reese's and rolo pretzel sandwiches

melted reese's or rolo in between two pretzel squares

recipe from Emily Miller


baked in May 2016

yield: 24 pretzel sandwiches
prep time: 5 minutes
cook time: 3 minutes
difficulty level: super easy

Ingredients
  • 48 pretzel squares (Snyder's snaps)
  • 12 rolos, unwrapped
  • 12 mini reese's, unwrapped

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place 24 pretzel squares on the baking sheet with one reese's or rolo on each.
  3. Place in the oven for 3 minutes.
  4. Place remaining pretzels on top of the candies, pushing down slightly.
  5. Let chocolate cool completely.

Nutrition

  • Calories per pretzel sandwich: 46
