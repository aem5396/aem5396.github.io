fantasy fudge

rich and creamy original fantasy fudge

recipe from recipe from Dear Crissy


baked in december 2024

yield: 32 pieces of fudge
prep time: 10 minutes
cook time: 10 minutes
difficulty level: hard



image from Dear Crissy

Ingredients
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup (1 and 1/2 sticks) butter
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 7-ounce jar marshmallow creme
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions
  1. Spray a 9×9 (or 9×13) pan with cooking spray.
  2. Next, line with parchment paper, leaving overhang on both sides so you can easily lift the fudge out when it’s done.
  3. In a medium pot, mix sugar, butter and milk.
  4. Bring the mixture to a full rolling boil using medium-high heat, stirring continuously to prevent scorching.
  5. Boil for exactly five minutes. This is important! Set a timer!
  6. Remove the mixture from the heat. Working quickly, stir in chocolate chips until smooth.
  7. Add the marshmallow creme and vanilla.
  8. Optionally, add nuts.
  9. Pour into the prepared pan; allow to cool completely before cutting into squares.
