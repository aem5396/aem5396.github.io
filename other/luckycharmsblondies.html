lucky charms blondies

blondies with cereal milk frosting topped with lucky charms marshmallows

recipe from Lily P Crumbs


baked in Februrary 2025

yield: 9
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 30 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients

Blondies

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, at room temperature
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour

Frosting

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups Lucky Charms cereal
  • 1 cup (120g) confectioners sugar, plus more as needed

Directions

For the blondies:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line an 8X8 pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl combine melted butter and brown sugar. Mix with a nonstick spatula until combined.
  3. Add in eggs + vanilla and mix again.
  4. In a separate smaller bowl, combine flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Pour into the lined 8X8 pan.
  7. Place in oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the edges are browned and the center looks slightly underdone.

For the frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, pour the cereal and the heavy cream. Let cereal soak for at least 30 minutes longer the better!
  2. Using a sieve, strain the heavy cream from the cereal into a large bowl.
  3. Add confectioner’s sugar to the strained heavy cream and beat using an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  4. Spread using an offset spatula onto cooled blondies.
  5. Scatter marshmallows on top and cut into squares!
