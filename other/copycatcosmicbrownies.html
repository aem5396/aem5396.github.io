copycat cosmic brownies

dense and rich brownie topped with creamy chocolate frosting and mini m&ms

recipe from Broma Bakery


baked in February 2023

yield: 16 brownies
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 18 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients

Brownie

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 5 Tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 eggs and 1 egg yolks, room temperature
  • 1 Tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Frosting

  • 1 and 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, room temperature
  • mini m&m's

Directions

For the brownies:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and line a 8 x 8 pan with parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until homogenous.
  4. Add the eggs, honey, and vanilla extract and whisk until combined, about 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the dry ingredients until no clumps of flour or cocoa remain.
  6. Pour the batter into your prepared pan and bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out mostly clean.
  7. Allow the brownies to cool.

For the frosting:

  1. In a double boiler, melt the chocolate chips, whisking constantly to make sure they do not burn.
  2. Slowly pour in the cream, whisking constantly until smooth and combined.
  3. Pour over the cooled brownies and sprinkles with mini m&m's.
  4. Let set for 20 minutes.
  5. Chill brownies in refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
