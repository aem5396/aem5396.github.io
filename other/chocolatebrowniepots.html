chocolate brownie pots

rich chocolate brownies for two baked in mini cocottes

recipe from The Baking Explorer


baked in December 2024

yield: 2 brownie pots
prep time: 20 minutes
cook time: 20 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients
  • 2.65 ounces dark chocolate
  • 3 Tablespoons + 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup light brown soft sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 Tablespoons + 1 teaspoon plain flour
  • 1 ounce chocolate chips (optional)

Directions
  1. Pre-heat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Put the dark chocolate and butter in a microwave safe bowl, microwave for 30 seconds, then stir. Keep microwaving in 10 second intervals, stirring between each one, until fully melted together.Set aside for 10 minutes to cool.
  3. Add the egg and light brown sugar to the bowl and whisk in well.
  4. Add the flour and whisk in, then fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Divide the mixture between the two mini cocottes.
  6. Place onto a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes, they will be slightly puffed up, crackled on top but still gooey in the center.
  7. Leave for 5 minutes so you don't burn your mouth, then serve warm with ice cream or any topping of your choice, there should not be any leftovers!
