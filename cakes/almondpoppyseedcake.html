almond poppy seed cake

moist almond poppy seed cake with vanilla cream cheese frosting

recipe from Ashlee Marie


baked in March 2020

yield: 24 servings
prep time: 30 minutes
cook time: 20 minutes
difficulty level: easy

Ingredients

Cake

  • 3 eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp vanilla
  • 1/2 Tbsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp butter emulsion
  • 1 and 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1-2 Tbsp poppy seeds

Frosting

  • 8 ounces full fat cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 4 Tbsp (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 Tbsp clear vanilla
  • 2 and 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 Tbsp heavy whipping cream

Directions

For the cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Hand whisk the eggs and sugar
  3. Add the flour, baking powder and salt and beat
  4. Add the vanilla, almond extract, butter emulsion, milk, and oil and beat until smooth
  5. Add the poppy seeds and fold in
  6. Pour into a well greased 13×18 inch jelly roll pan
  7. Cook for 20 minutes, then let cool in the pan

For the frosting:

  1. Beat the cream cheese, butter, and clear vanilla together until fluffy
  2. Add the powdered sugar and beat until smooth and slightly thick
  3. Add the heavy cream a tsp at a time and beat until the desired consistency is achieved
  4. Frost the cake and enjoy!
